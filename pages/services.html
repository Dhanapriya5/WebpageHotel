Our Services

At our hotel, we provide a wide range of services to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay for our guests.

Restaurant & Dining

Experience gourmet dining at our on-site restaurant, offering a variety of local and international cuisines. Enjoy a meal in a sophisticated atmosphere with top-notch service.

  • Breakfast, lunch, and dinner options

Relax and rejuvenate at our full-service spa. We offer a range of treatments including massages, facials, and body therapies. Our wellness facilities include a sauna, steam room, and a fitness center.

  • Wide range of treatments
  • Sauna and steam room
  • Fully equipped fitness center

Conference & Meeting Rooms

Our hotel features modern conference and meeting rooms equipped with the latest technology. Perfect for business meetings, conferences, and events. We also offer catering services and event planning assistance.

  • Multiple meeting rooms

  • Event planning and catering services

Swimming Pool

Take a dip in our outdoor swimming pool. Relax by the poolside with comfortable loungers and umbrellas. Poolside service is available for drinks and snacks.

  • Outdoor pool

Free Wi-Fi

Stay connected with our complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi available throughout the hotel. Whether you're in your room, the lobby, or the restaurant, you can enjoy seamless internet access.

  • High-speed internet
  • Available throughout the hotel
